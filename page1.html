July 20th, 1914

8 days before WW1

Today I arrived at ellis island.
Some people call it the Isle of terrors,
but I much rather like the Isle of Hope better.
I plan on starting from the ground up.
I'm going to become a factory man,
and mabye someday I'll be rich and sucsessful.
My name is Andrew Johnson, and i'm from great brittan.

July 22nd, 1914

6 days before WW1

Right now I'm staying with family. My brother
Anthony came a few years before me,
so he's letting me stay with him for a while.
recently, there's been talk of war. A big one too,
I sure hope thats not the case.
